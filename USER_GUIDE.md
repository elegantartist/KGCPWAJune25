# Keep Going Care - Patient User Guide

Welcome to Keep Going Care (KGC)! This guide will walk you through the key features of the app and how to use them to support your health journey.

## 1. Getting Started: Logging In

You will receive a welcome email from your doctor with a link to access the KGC app. The first time you log in, you will need to set up your account and password.

## 2. The Dashboard

The Dashboard is your home screen. From here, you can quickly access the most important features:

*   **Chat with your Health Assistant:** Opens the AI-powered chatbot.
*   **Log Daily Scores:** Takes you to the screen to record your daily self-scores.
*   **Keep Going Button:** Activates the "Keep Going Sequence" to help you stay motivated.

## 3. Daily Self-Scores

Every day, you'll be prompted to log your self-scores for:

*   **Healthy Meal Plan:** How well you followed your diet goals.
*   **Exercise & Wellness:** How well you followed your exercise and wellness plan.
*   **Prescription Medication:** How well you adhered to your medication schedule.

Use the sliders to select a score from 1 to 10 for each category. This information is shared with your doctor to help them understand your progress.

## 4. Chat with your Health Assistant

Your AI Health Assistant is here to help you 24/7. You can ask it questions about:

*   Healthy meal ideas
*   Exercise suggestions
*   Information about your medications
*   Tips for staying motivated

The assistant's advice will always be based on the Care Plan Directives (CPDs) set by your doctor.

## 5. The "Keep Going" Sequence

If you're feeling stressed, tempted, or unmotivated, press the "Keep Going" button on the dashboard. This will start a sequence designed to help you refocus:

1.  Your personal motivational image will be displayed.
2.  A short, calming video will play.
3.  A guided breathing exercise will begin.

## 6. Motivational Image

You can upload and personalize your own motivational image in the "Motivation" section. This is the image that will appear during the "Keep Going" sequence. You can even add fun effects like sparkling stars to make it your own!

## 7. Finding Inspiration

KGC has several features to help you find new ideas:

*   **Inspiration Machine - Diet:** Get recipe and meal ideas that fit your diet plan.
*   **Inspiration Machine - Exercise & Wellness:** Find new exercise routines and wellness activities.
*   **Food Database:** Look up nutritional information for different foods.

## 8. Progress Milestones

As you consistently log high daily self-scores, you'll earn badges and rewards! Check the "Progress Milestones" section to see what you can achieve.

## 9. Journaling

The Journal is your private space to write down your thoughts, feelings, and experiences throughout your health journey.

We hope this guide is helpful. Remember, Keep Going!
